Go! Go! Nippon! Live With You!

MangaGamer

We’re excited to announce our virtual YouTuber project, Go! Go! Nippon! Live With You! Go! Go! Nippon! Live With You! is a spinoff of the series of the same name. The game’s heroine sisters, Makoto and Akira Misaki, are here to whisk you away on livestream adventures, be it to a virtual karaoke box, a dangerous dungeon, or even down the everyday streets of Tokyo!

Distant Memoraĵo

Sukera Suparo

In Summer 2019, MangaGamer and SukeraSparo brought you The Expression: Amrilato, a heartwarming, and educational yuri visual novel that teaches readers Esperanto, as well as the meaning of communication. Now comes Distant Memoraĵo—two new chapters in the tale of Rin and Ruka.

Horny Elves & A Moral Orc

Waffle

New from MangaGamer and Waffle, the creators of Funbag Fantasy, comes Horny Elves and a Moral Orc, the eroge adaptation of Tomokity’s hentai manga and Youtube franchise!

Christmas Tina

NekoDay

Christmas Tina is a story that focuses on the struggle of those less fortunate in a wealthy society. These are working class people, struggling to save money for important medical bills or immigrants hoping to go to university one day, set against the backdrop of the Japanese economic bubble of the late 1980s.
