Q-Gaku

Q-Gaku is an eroge character designer, colorist, illustrator, and designer working primarily in titles targeted toward adult males. In his younger days, he wanted to draw artwork for all kinds of genres, so he spent several years in the anime industry honing his skills. Now he’s most famous for the Funbag Fantasy series and Eden’s Ritter.
