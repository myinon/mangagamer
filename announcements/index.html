Important Information Regarding Anime Expo Autograph Sessions

  • A ticket is required to participate in the autograph sessions.
  • Autograph tickets are guest and session specific (e.g. one ticket may be for Ryukishi 07 session #1, etc).
  • Tickets may be obtained upon request after spending $50 or more at the MangaGamer Booth (Kentia Hall #1213).
  • Autograph tickets are limited to one per customer per guest. Customers who spend over $100 at the MG booth may obtain one ticket for each guest (if availability permits).
  • Tickets are limited and will be offered on a first-come-first-serve basis.
  • Autograph sessions are limited to a maximum of two items. Guests will only sign blank shikishi, guest-related official hardcopies, or character shikishi sold at the MG booth.
  • Due to time constraints no art commissions, photos, or videos will be permitted.
