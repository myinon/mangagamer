Ran Yamakaze

MOONSTONE

Yamakaze Ran is the lead concept artist, graphic designer, and illustrator at MOONSTONE. Itaru Hinoue

As a character designer and illustrator, Itaru Hinoue is an iconic figure within otaku culture. With popular titles such as Rewrite, Little Busters, and Clannad among her accomplishments, she easily stands out amidst her contemporaries in the industry.
