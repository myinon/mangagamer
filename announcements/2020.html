Trinoline Genesis

minori

A true treat for Trinoline fans! Trinoline Genesis features all-new stories for the original three heroines—Sara, SHIRONE, and Yuuri—and adds two brand-new routes for the sub-heroines of the original game—Ayaka and Hanako!

Jeanne at the Clock Tower
～Jeanne a la tour d’horloge～

liarsoft

In the final days of WWII, a French soldier, Roland, and his sworn friend Olivier are seperated from their unit and attacked by a band of ghastly medieval knights. Roland is fatally wounded in protecting Olivier, but awakens in a calm forest, tended to by a lovely girl—Jeanne. He wonders if the attack was merely a dream, but it was no such thing, and the world he has found himself in is not the one he remembers either…

Steam Prison - Fin Route

HuneX

In this expansion to Steam Prison, fans are finally able to enjoy a route with Cyrus’s kind, devoted coworker, Fin! In this newly added tale, Fin is the one who takes the blame for the crime you’re falsely accused of, and you find your roles reversed, dynamically shifting the course of events for both characters! In addition, this new expansion also includes several new bonus side stories for the rest of the cast!
